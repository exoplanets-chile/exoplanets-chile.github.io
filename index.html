News

Welcome to a growing entry point for exoplanetary research in Chile. This page connects community news, the ExoChile2026 meeting, the XO-Chile searchable community database, and useful links for students and researchers.

The site is meant to remain simple and easy to update as the Chilean exoplanet community develops new meetings, tools, initiatives, and collaborations.
